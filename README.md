### 📌 Project Executive Summary
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify peak operating hours and top-performing product categories. The objective was to provide the shop manager with data-backed insights to reduce waste and ensure high-demand products are always in stock during "rush hours."

### 🛠️ Technical Workflow & Tools
- **Microsoft Excel:** Developed a multi-tab analytical tool with automated Pivot Tables.
- **Data Cleaning:** Standardized date/time formats and handled missing values in the transaction log to ensure accurate time-series reporting.
- **Visual Design:** Created a clean, "Management-Ready" dashboard using conditional formatting and dynamic charts.

### 📈 Strategic Business Insights
- **The "Evening Surge":** Data revealed that peak sales occur during **evening hours**, contradicting the assumption that mornings were the only high-traffic window.
- **Revenue Drivers:** Coffee-based products contribute **over 60% of total revenue**, identifying them as the primary focus for loyalty programs.
- **Weekend Dynamics:** Sales volume increases by **[X]% on weekends**, suggesting a need for increased staffing levels on Saturdays and Sundays.
- **Menu Optimization:** Identified low-performing items that contribute less than 2% to total sales, providing a list for potential removal or promotion.
